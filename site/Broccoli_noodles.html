Broccoli_noodles

4 Portions - 40 Minutes

A quick and cheap hearthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 450g Udon noodles
  • one head of broccoli
  • fresh chilli peppers
  • one large white onion
  • half a clove of garlic
  • soy sauce
  • honey
  • dijon mustard

Preperation:

  • Wash and cut one head of broccoli. Take a large pot with water and a bit of salt and wait for it to boil. Add broccoli to it and cook until soft. (3-4min) While cutting the broccoli there will be small particles of it left, add that to a frying pan.

  • Cut one big white onion and add to a frying pan, with some oil of your choosing. Cut 3 cihlli peppers into smallest size you can, and add it into frying pan. When onions are close to ready, chope a couple of cloves of garlic and add it.

  • By this time broccoli will be ready. Pour the water out.

  • For the sauce you will need some soy souce, honey and Dejon mustard. Add all of the ingredients together in a bowl and mix it. (60%soysouce, 20%honey, 20%Dejon mustard)

  • Start making the noodles. You can use any kind you want, but i prefear to use dried Udon noodles. Use 4 portion sizes.

  • Add the broccoli to a pan with onions, chili and garlic. Keep it on a low heat and add sauce. We want to thicken it.

  • When the noodles are done cooking add them to the pan, mix it and cook it for a couple of minutes, till all of the ingredients got to know eatchother.

  • Garnish with chopped fresh spring onions

  • Enjoy it with frends.
